ARTICLE 3
Limitation of Director Liability

To the fullest extent that the Oregon Business Corporation Act, as it exists on the date
hereof or may hereafter be amended, permits the limitation or elimination of the liability of
directors, a director of the corporation shall not be liable to the corporation or its shareholders for
any monetary damages for conduct as a director. Any amendment to or repeal of this Article or
amendment to the Oregon Business Corporation Act shall not adversely affect any right or
protection of a director of the corporation for or with respect to any acts or omissions of such
director occurring prior to such amendment or repeal.
