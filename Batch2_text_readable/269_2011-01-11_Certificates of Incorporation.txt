CERTIFICATE OF AMENDMENT
OF
RESTATED CERTIFICATE OF INCORPORATION
OF

AGGREGATE KNOWLEDGE INC.

Aggregate Knowledge inc., a Delaware corporation, does hereby certify that the
following amendment to the corporation's Restated Certificate of Incorporation has been duly
adopted in accordance with the provisions of Section 242 of the Delaware General Corporation
Law, with the approval of such amendment by the corporation's stockholders having been given
by written consent without a meeting in accordance with Sections 228(d) and 242 of the
Delaware General Corporation Law:

Article IV of the Restated Certificate of Incorporation, relating to authorized shares is
hereby amended and restated to read in its entirety as follows:

“This corporation is authorized to issue two (2) classes of shares, designated “Common
Stock” and “Preferred Stock.” The total number of shares of Common Stock authorized to be
issued is 68,750,000 shares, $0.001 par value per share. The total number of shares of Preferred
Stock authorized to be issued is 43,721,584 shares, $0.001 par value per share, 6,429,794 of
which are designated as “Series A Preferred Stock”, 4,673,401 of which are designated as
“Series B Preferred Stock” and 32,618,389 of which are designated as “Series C Preferred
Stock”.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, said corporation has caused this Certificate of Amendment to
be signed by its duly authorized officer this 11th day of January 2011 the foregoing facts stated
herein are true and correct.
