ATHLETES’ PERFORMANCE, INC.

Athletes’ Performance, Inc., a corporation organized and existing under and by virtue of the
General Corporation Law of the State of Delaware (the “Corporation”), does hereby certify as

follows:

FIRST: That, by written action of the Board of Directors of the Corporation, resolutions were duly
adopted, proposing and declaring advisable that the Amended and Restated Certificate of
Incorporation of the Corporation, be amended and that such amendments be submitted to
the stockholders of the Corporation for their consideration, as follows:

RESOLVED: That the Board of Directors of the Corporation recommends and deems it
