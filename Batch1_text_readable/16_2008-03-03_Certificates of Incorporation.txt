CERTIFICATE OF AMENDMENT

OF THE RESTATED CERTIFICATE OF INCORPORATION
OF M-FACTOR, INC.

M-Factor, Inc. (the “Company”), a corporation organized and existing
under and by virtue of the General Corporation Law of the State of Delaware (the
“General Corporation Law”),

DOES HEREBY CERTIFY:

FIRST: That the name of the Company is M-Factor, Inc. and that the
Company was originally incorporated pursuant to the General Corporation Law on July 3,
2003 under the name 3-D Marketing Technologies, Inc.

SECOND: That the date on which the Restated Certificate of
Incorporation of the Company was filed with the Secretary of State of the State of
Delaware is May 16, 2007.

THIRD: That the Board of Directors of the Company adopted a resolution
setting forth a proposed amendment to the Restated Certificate of Incorporation, declaring
said amendment to be advisable and in the best interests of the Company, and authorizing
the appropriate officers of the Company to solicit the consent of the stockholders therefor,
which resolution setting forth the proposed amendment is as follows:

“RESOLVED, that that Article 1V.B.3.(d)Gi)(H) of the Restated
Certificate of Incorporation of the Company be amended to read in its
entirety as follows:

(H) Common Stock issued to lease companies,
real estate lessors, banks, financial institutions or other
entities with which the Company has business
relationships in connection with commercial credit
atrangements, equipment financings, leases or other
transactions that in each case has been approved by this
corporation’s Board of Directors (including unanimous
approval of the Preferred Directors, as defined below),
provided such issuances are for other than primarily
equity financing purposes.”

FOURTH: That thereafter said amendment was duly adopted in
accordance with the provisions of Section 242 of the General Corporation Law by written
consent of the stockholders holding the requisite number of shares required by statute
given in accordance with and pursuant to Section 228 of the General Corporation Law of
the State of Delaware.
