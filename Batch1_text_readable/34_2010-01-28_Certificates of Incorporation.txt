4Home, Inc. (the “Corporation”), a corporation duly organized and existing under the
General Corporation Law of the State of Delaware (the “DGCL”), DOES HEREBY CERTIFY:

1. The name of the Corporation is 4Home, Inc. and the Certificate of Incorporation
of the Corporation was filed with the Secretary of State of Delaware on February 8, 2006 under

below has been duly adopted in accordance with the provisions of Section 242 of the DGCL, and

Section 228 of the DGCL.

amended to read in its entirety as follows:
