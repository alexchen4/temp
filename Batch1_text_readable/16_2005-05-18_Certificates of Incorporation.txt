OF 3-D MARKETING TECHNOLOGIES, INC.

3-D Marketing Technologies, Inc., a corporation organized and existing
under and by virtue of the General Corporation Law of the State of Delaware (the
“Corporation”)

DOES HEREBY CERTIFY:
FIRST: The name of the Corporation is 3-D Marketing Technologies, Inc.

Corporation was originally filed with the Secretary of State of the State of Delaware is
July 3, 2003, under the same name.

same name.

resolution setting forth a proposed amendment to the Restated Certificate of
Incorporation, declaring said amendment to be advisable and in the best interests of the
Corporation:

“RESOLVED, that ArticleI of the Restated Certificate of
Incorporation of the Company be amended to read in its entirety as
follows:

The name of this corporation is M-Factor, Inc.”

FIFTH: That thereafter said amendment was duly adopted in accordance
with the provisions of Section 242 of the General Corporation Law by written consent of
the stockholders holding the requisite number of shares required by statute given in
accordance with and pursuant to Section 228 of the General Corporation Law of the State
of Delaware.
